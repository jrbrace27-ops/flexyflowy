About FlexFlow

FlexFlow exists to help local contractors look more professional online and capture more job requests without promising fake “AI intelligence” or bloated custom systems.

Built for contractors

The product is designed around how real home-service businesses operate: urgent calls, missed voicemails, inconsistent web inquiries, and limited time.

Focused on conversion

Every page, widget, and intake flow is meant to make it easier for a customer to explain the job and easier for the contractor to respond.

Kept intentionally lean

No giant CRM promises. No fake automation empire. Just a service that helps contractors capture and organize more job inquiries, especially after hours.

Live demo: guided contractor intake

This demo is built to act like a real intake assistant. It stores what the customer already said, asks only for the next missing detail, and stops repeating itself.

Why this feels better than a generic chatbot

Instead of spitting out canned paragraphs, the demo bot moves through a structured intake flow. It remembers the user’s name, phone, address, urgency, and issue type, then asks for only the missing pieces.

  • Remembers captured details instead of repeating the same question
  • Separates emergency requests from routine quote requests
  • Collects structured intake data contractors can actually use
  • Ends with a clean summary the business could route by email

Suggested test prompt: “My furnace died at 14 Main Street in Buffalo. My name is John and my phone number is 716-555-1111.”
